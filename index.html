Who I Am

Prince Williams: Software Engineer

I am a software engineer, recently graduating and earning certification from WeCanCodeIT Software developer bootcamp in the following broad range of programming languages, concepts and design principles: C# || .NET || Active Server Pages / ASP.net || Javascript || jQuery || HTML5 || CSS3 || Bootstrap || MVC || Responsive design / Mobile || Structured Query Language (SQL) || Transactional Databases/MSSQL || Object Oriented || Source Control / Git

I believe my previous professional and educational experiences in Business Administration and Professional School Counseling, uniquely enable me to contribute in a number of significant ways to any software development team. I have worked extensively in team based and administrative capacities throughout my career and educational experiences. As a developer and team member I bring elite interpersonal and communication skills along with my growing coding abilities. I am above all ready to learn and take on new challenges to grow as a developer and embrace all opportunities to learn new skills and techniques!

I believe through technology all of my interests, passion and skills can be intertwined and stimulated. I am passionate about empowering disenfranchised people through the power of technology; enabling them to transform their lives in all aspects! I am determined to leverage my recently acquired technological skills in conjunction with my interpersonal skills, and previous career and academic experiences to actualize change in my place of work and the greater community as a whole! I am especially interested in augmented reality, virtual reality, wearable technology and new emerging technologies.
